About OpenSpace Tech Community

We are a student-led initiative dedicated to creating an inclusive tech-driven environment at UNICROSS. Our mission is to foster creativity, innovation, and skill development through collaboration and mentorship.

Our Core Values

  • Inclusiveness: Everyone is welcome regardless of background.
  • Collaboration: We build and grow together.
  • Innovation: We explore new ideas and technologies.
  • Mentorship: We grow through shared knowledge and guidance.
  • Leadership: We empower others and encourage ownership of ideas and teams.
