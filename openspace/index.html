Explore Our Learning Tracks

At Open Space, our community is organized into specialized learning tracks that help members grow in their areas of interest while collaborating on real-world projects.

  • Engineering Track: Focuses on coding, software development, Web3, cybersecurity, and systems engineering.
  • Creative Track: Covers UI/UX design, game art, digital media, and innovative tech experiences.
  • Project Track: Encourages collaboration through project building, feedback sessions, and shared learning challenges.
