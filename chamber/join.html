Non-Profit Membership

Our Non-Profit Membership is designed for organizations that work towards social good. This free membership provides access to networking opportunities, community events, and exposure to potential collaborators.

  • No membership fees
  • Access to networking events
  • Free directory listing

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership is perfect for small businesses looking to expand their network. It provides fundamental benefits, including business exposure and event invitations.

  • Basic networking opportunities
  • Invitations to chamber events
  • Business listing on our website

Silver Membership

Silver Membership is designed for businesses that want increased visibility and additional advertising perks.

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Enhanced advertising & directory placement
  • Discounts on events and promotions
×

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership offers VIP benefits, exclusive sponsorship opportunities, and premium exposure.

  • All Silver benefits
  • Premium directory placement
  • Featured advertising on homepage
  • Sponsorship opportunities & event recognition
